Privacy Policy for Local Habit

Effective date: 2025-07-01

Local Habit ("the App") is a privacy-first habit tracking application for Android and iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information. In summary:

  • Your habit data stays on your device.
  • Weather data is fetched only when you explicitly use the weather feature.
  • You have full control over your data and privacy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Habit and Personal Data

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Habit names, descriptions, and completion data you create
  • Daily completion streaks and statistics
  • Bundle/routine configurations
  • App theme and settings preferences
  • Local timestamps for habit tracking

1.2 Weather Data (Optional Feature)

When you use the weather feature, the App makes requests to Open-Meteo weather services:

  • Weather API: api.open-meteo.com - for current and forecast weather data
  • Geocoding API: geocoding-api.open-meteo.com - to convert location names to coordinates
  • Weather data includes temperature, humidity, precipitation, and sunrise/sunset times
  • Weather data is cached locally and automatically expires

1.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No advertising, analytics, or crash-reporting data
  • No device identifiers, contacts, SMS, phone access
  • No camera or microphone access
  • No background location tracking or GPS coordinates
  • No account creation or user authentication

2. Data Storage & Processing

  • All habit data is stored locally using Isar database in the App's private storage
  • Weather data is temporarily cached locally and expires automatically
  • On Android, data resides in app-specific directory (/Android/data/com.localhabit.localhabit/)
  • No cloud backup, synchronization, or remote servers for habit data
  • Data is only accessible by the App and not shared with other apps

3. Network Access & Third-Party Services

3.1 Weather Services

The App optionally connects to Open-Meteo services when you use weather features:

  • Purpose: Fetch weather data for your specified location
  • Data Sent: Geographic coordinates or location name you provide
  • Data Received: Weather forecasts, current conditions, sunrise/sunset times
  • Privacy: Open-Meteo is a privacy-focused service with no tracking or personal data collection

3.2 No Other Third-Party Integrations

The App contains no advertisements, analytics SDKs, or other third-party services.

5. Weather Feature Privacy

  • User Control: Weather features are entirely optional and user-initiated
  • Location Data: You manually enter location names or coordinates; no automatic location detection
  • Data Retention: Weather data is cached locally for performance and expires automatically
  • Network Requests: Only made when you explicitly request weather information
  • No Tracking: Open-Meteo services do not track users or collect personal information

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages including children under 13. Because minimal data is collected and no personal information is transmitted, it complies with COPPA requirements.

7. Security

We implement security best practices:

  • Local data is protected by Android/iOS app sandboxing
  • Network requests use HTTPS encryption
  • No sensitive data is logged or transmitted
  • Your device security (screen lock, encryption) protects your habit data

8. Your Choices & Control

  • Habit Data: Create, edit, delete habits and completions at any time
  • Weather Features: Use weather features only when you choose
  • Location Control: Manually enter locations; no automatic location access
  • App Settings: Customize themes and preferences
  • Data Removal: Uninstalling the App permanently removes all local data
  • Network Control: Disable internet to use the App entirely offline (habit tracking will work normally, weather features will not)

9. Offline Functionality

The core habit tracking features work completely offline:

  • Creating and managing habits
  • Tracking daily completions
  • Viewing streaks and statistics
  • Using bundles/routines
  • All app settings and customization

Only the optional weather feature requires internet connectivity.

10. International Data Transfers

Weather data requests to Open-Meteo services may involve international data transfers as these services operate globally. No personal or habit data is involved in these transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect App changes or legal requirements. Updated policies will be posted in-app and on our website with a new "Effective date."
