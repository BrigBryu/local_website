Privacy-first habit tracking with multiple habit types, bundles, and local storage

In Development

Local Habit is currently being developed with a focus on privacy, flexibility, and offline functionality.

Current Features in Development:

  • Multiple habit types (Basic, Bundle, Stack, Avoidance, Interval, Weekly)
  • Habit bundles for grouping related habits
  • Weekly habits with custom day selection
  • Beautiful dark theme with teal/cyan accents
  • Local SQLite storage - no cloud required
  • Streak tracking and progress visualization
  • Multiple theme options (Vim-inspired color schemes)
  • Completely offline functionality

Planned Features

⚡ Multiple Habit Types

Basic daily habits, weekly routines, habit stacks, avoidance tracking, and interval-based habits for maximum flexibility.

📦 Habit Bundles

Group related habits into bundles like "morning routine" or "workout session" for better organization and tracking.

📅 Weekly Habits

Set habits for specific days of the week with custom scheduling and "due in X days" notifications.

🔥 Streak Tracking

Visual progress tracking with streak counters and completion statistics to keep you motivated.

🔒 Complete Privacy

All data stored locally in SQLite. No accounts, no analytics, no cloud syncing. Your habits stay private.

🎨 Beautiful Themes

Multiple color themes including Dracula, Gruvbox, Nord, Solarized, and more for a personalized experience.
