Privacy Policy for Local Notes

Effective date: 2025-01-15

Local Notes ("the App") is a privacy-first, entirely offline note-taking application for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information. In short:

  • No data ever leaves your device.
  • The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal or device information.
  • You are always in full control of your notes.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information. The App does not request, log, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting data
  • Contacts, location, camera, microphone, or unique device identifiers

The only data stored is the content you create (text notes, tags, timestamps) plus minimal technical metadata inside the on-device SQLite database.

2. Data Storage & Processing

  • All notes and settings are stored locally in the App's private storage sandbox using SQLite database
  • The database uses FTS5 (Full-Text Search) for local search functionality and is never transmitted
  • On Android, the database resides in the app-specific data directory (/Android/data/com.brigbryu.localnotes/)
  • No cloud backup, synchronization, or internet connectivity is used

3. Export / Import Features

The App offers explicit, user-initiated export/import features:

  • Export: Creates .zip archives containing your notes as .txt files, saved to your device's local storage
  • Import: Reads .txt, .md, .json, or .zip files you select from your device
  • All export/import operations are entirely local and user-controlled
  • The App does not automatically upload, send, or share exported files

4. Permissions Used

The App requests no sensitive permissions (e.g., SMS, Contacts, Location, Camera, Microphone).

5. Ads & Third-Party Services

The App contains no advertisements and integrates no third-party SDKs, analytics, or cloud services.

6. Internet & Network Access

The App operates entirely offline and does not require or use internet connectivity. No network permissions are requested.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. Because no data is collected or transmitted, it complies with COPPA by default.

8. Security

We follow Android platform best practices to safeguard on-device data. Your device security (screen lock, encryption, OS updates) protects your notes.

9. Your Choices & Control

  • Create / Edit / Delete: Manage notes at any time within the App
  • Export: Save notes to accessible device storage when you choose
  • Import: Add notes from files you select
  • Uninstall: Removing the App permanently deletes all local data

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. Updated policies will be posted in-app and on our website with a new "Effective date."
